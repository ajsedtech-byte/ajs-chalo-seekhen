🎯 1. Vision & Problem Statement

The Problem

Zero Digital Presence
Local shopkeepers invisible online — customers don't know what's available nearby
Unpredictable Schedules
Vegetable sellers come and go — customers waste time visiting empty carts
No Pre-Booking
Fresh vegetables gone before you arrive — no way to reserve in advance
Can't Compete
Small shops losing to Amazon/Flipkart — need a hyperlocal platform

The Vision

AJ's Bazaar gives every Mhow shopkeeper a digital storefront in minutes. Customers discover, browse, and order from nearby shops. Introduces vegetable pre-booking — reserve your sabzi before it reaches the market. Supports self-pickup and delivery. Runs on UPI + Cash on Delivery.

🥬 8. Pre-Booking System — Deep Dive

Complete Flow

Day Before (Evening 4-8 PM)

SELLER
1. Posts tomorrow's vegetable list
— Names, photos, estimated prices
3. Receives booking notifications
CUSTOMER
2. Browses available vegetables
— Selects items, qty, pickup slot
4. Gets confirmation "Prices finalize by 7 AM"

Booking Day (Morning 5-7 AM)

SELLER
5. Goes to mandi, buys based on bookings
6. Confirms final prices
CUSTOMER
7. Gets price notification
8. Confirms, modifies, or cancels (free)

Pickup/Delivery Window

SELLER
9. Packs pre-booked orders
12. Marks completed, payment received
CUSTOMER
10. Gets "order ready" notification
11. Picks up or receives delivery
13. Rates the experience

Business Rules

⏰ Timing

  • Post availability: 4-10 PM (day before)
  • Booking window: Until 10 PM
  • Price confirmation: 5-7 AM
  • Customer confirm/modify: 7-8 AM
  • Auto-cancel unconfirmed: 8:30 AM

💰 Pricing

  • Estimated price set by seller
  • Tolerance: ±20% from estimate
  • >20% increase: free cancel option
  • Price drops: auto lower price

📦 Quantity

  • Min: 250g per vegetable
  • Steps: 250g, 500g, 1kg, 2kg, 5kg
  • Max per item: 10kg
  • Seller can cap total bookings

function generateUPILink(params) { const url = new URL('upi://pay'); url.searchParams.set('pa', params.sellerUpiId); // "raju@upi" url.searchParams.set('pn', params.shopName); url.searchParams.set('am', params.amount.toString()); url.searchParams.set('cu', 'INR'); url.searchParams.set('tn', `AJ's Bazaar Order ${params.orderId}`); return url.toString(); }

🔒 14. Security

Authentication

Phone OTP (Supabase Auth) • JWT with role claims • Session refresh tokens • Rate limit: 5 OTP/hour

Authorization

Row Level Security (RLS) • Sellers modify own shop only • Customers see own orders only • Admin-only routes

Data Protection

All HTTPS • Encrypted at rest • Phone numbers masked • UPI IDs restricted • No card data stored

Input Validation

Zod schemas for all API inputs • SQL injection prevented • XSS prevented • File upload validation

Rate Limiting

API: 100 req/min • Search: 30 req/min • Orders: 5/min • OTP: 5/hour per phone

Seller Verification

Phone verified • Shop photo review • Aadhaar/PAN optional • Geolocation check

💰 15. Monetization

Phase 1: Launch (First 6 months) — FREE

Goal: 50+ sellers, 500+ customers. Zero commission, zero fees. Build trust in Mhow.
